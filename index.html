Welcome to My Portfolio

I am Irith Chaturvedi, a junior in the Data Science and Statistics program at the University of Illinois. As an international student from New Delhi, India, I have developed an adaptability and curiosity that drive me to explore global opportunities. My experiences span both technical and leadership domains. As an undergraduate Research Assistant at the university’s Biodiversity Genomics Lab, I worked under Dr. Milton Tan to reconstruct the Tree of Life for fish phylogenies, applying data-driven techniques to complex biological datasets, handling novel issues of gene duplication and hybridization using a normalized reference scoring method developed by me. Additionally, I have undertaken independent projects analyzing the impact of COVID-19 on U.S. pharmaceutical stock prices by integrating and cleaning datasets from the Alpha Vantage API and Johns Hopkins University, leveraging Python, SQL, and OpenRefine for data processing, and automating workflows using Snakemake. I also designed a machine learning model to identify affordable housing options in U.S. Northeast, integrating spatial visualizations and predictive analytics to recommend neighborhoods and properties based on family size, budget, and proximity to schools and, have projects involving computer vision by creating an image recognition program using PyTorch, deciphering alphabets, numbers and objects using a CNN with 98.5% accuracy. I am also a part of the Course team at the Department of Statistics at the University of Illinois - as a Course Assistant for the STAT 420 course on Regression modeling and application with instructor Dr. Julie Deeke. Beyond academics, I have interned at the 21st Century Dads Foundation in Chicago, where I managed a database of over a thousand families worldwide, optimized engagement through data visualization, and helped develop the foundation’s mobile application. My role as Social Media Lead for the Dads Honor Ride, a fundraiser supporting families raising children with special needs, enabled me to blend data-driven decision-making with strategic outreach, ultimately helping raise $80,000. Furthermore, I secured $120,000 in marketing and advertising funds from a single grant maker, demonstrating my ability to translate analytical insights into tangible outcomes.
